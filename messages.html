    📋 One-time Formspree Setup
    1. Go to formspree.io and create a free account (50 submissions/month free).
    2. Click + New Form → name it "Wedding Messages".
    3. Copy your form endpoint — it looks like: https://formspree.io/f/xyzabcde
    4. Open js/config.js, find messages.formspreeEndpoint, and paste your endpoint there.
    5. Delete this yellow box from messages.html once the form is live.
